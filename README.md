# LeNet-5
CIFAR10 (at least 60% test accuracy) and MNIST(at least 99% test accuracy)

Stochastic gradient descent method applies in this model. 

For CIFAR10:
* 30 epochs
* Learning rate schedule by 10 every 10 epochs
* Weight decay
* Data normalization

For MNIST:
* 20 epochs
* Learning rate schedule by 10 every 10 epochs
* Weight decay
